History of FootBall

The history of this game dates back to more than 2500 years BC, where the ancient Chinese played it. They would provide banquets to the winning team and flog the losing team. The Greeks and Japanese knew it 600 years BC, and the Egyptians 300 years BC. However this game, the way they use to play it today, emerged in England. In the year 1016 AD.
